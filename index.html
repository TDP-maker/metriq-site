Questions VAs ask us

Will my client know I used a tool?

No. The report carries your logo, your business name and your colours. There is no Metriq branding anywhere on it.

Do I need to own the ad account?

No. Partner or admin access to the client's account is enough, which is what you already have to run their ads.

What if I only run organic, not ads?

Metriq reads Meta ads accounts right now. If you don't run paid ads for a client, this isn't the right tool for that account yet.

How do I pay?

Card at checkout, including a GCash Mastercard. Billed monthly in US dollars, cancel any time from your settings.

Who is behind Metriq?

Metriq is built by The Digital Peach, a Meta Business Partner agency that runs client ad accounts every day. When you connect an account you will see The Peach System named in the Facebook permission screen, which is our platform.

Is my client's account safe?

Metriq only reads the account. It never changes campaigns, budgets or ads, and it cannot post or spend. You make every change yourself in Ads Manager.

Can I change the wording before I send it?

Yes. Open the report in your browser, edit any section, then download the PDF.
