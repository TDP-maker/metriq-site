Frame rules the ones that bite
Transparent PNG, 1:1. The frame is stretched to each square tile. Any ratio other than 1:1 distorts. Export it exactly square.
Keep the centre clear. Meta drops the product into the middle. Anything opaque near the centre hides the product.
One frame, every product. You get a single overlay for the whole set. It has to work on your darkest and your lightest product, not just the hero.
Edges are where it bites. A heavy border eats into products that already fill the frame. Leave breathing room so nothing important sits under the border.
Test on real products. A frame that looks perfect on the hero often clips a busy or edge-to-edge product. That is the whole point of this preview.
Hero can be image or video in the real ad. Tiles pull dynamically from the catalog, so you cannot control which product lands where.
